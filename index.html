Problems faced:

  • problems include antomical complexity of spine
  • MRI is often able to capture good quality images of soft tissue, but bone are sometimes captured with intensity similar to air
  • Images contain noise

Plan of action:

  • we can reduce the size of the image (decrease number of pixels) and filter the image using method proposed in paper proposed by Nobi and Yousuf (2011)
  • Decided to impletement Guassian Filter as this is exists in Simple ITK
  • Began research on how method would be tested. This data set containing 10 images could be used to evaluate efficacy of an algorithm
